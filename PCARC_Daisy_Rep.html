Daisy Repeater Info

145.210

The Daisy 145.210 repeater is located on Bald Mountain in southern Pittsburg county.  "210" is built from a 110W continuous duty GE Mastr III repeater station.  Control, IDs, courtesy tones, etc. are provided by the Audio Test Solutions RLC Club Deluxe controller, which also provides control for the 442.400 repeater. Automated weather alerts are provided by the CAT WX-250 SAME weather alert receiver.  Even though 210 and 400 share the same controller, both repeaters can be separated with independent IDs, timers, etc., with the WX-250 being the only thing in common. 442.400

442.400

The Daisy 442.400 repeater is located on Bald Mountain in southern Pittsburg county and serves as the hub for our linked repeater system. "400" is built from a 75W continuous duty Motorola Micor repeater station.  Control, IDs, courtesy tones, etc. are provided by the Audio Test Solutions RLC Club Deluxe controller, which also provides control for the 145.210 repeater. Automated weather alerts are provided by the CAT WX-250 SAME weather alert receiver.  Even though 400 and 210 share the same controller, both repeaters can be separated with independent IDs, timers, etc., with the WX-250 being the only thing in common.
