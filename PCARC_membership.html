PCARC Membership

All persons interested in Amateur Radio shall be eligible for membership. Classifications and privileges of membership shall be as follows:

DUES PAYING:

Set at $40.00 a year, per person. Payable at the first meeting of the year in January, with the option first ½ ($20.00) coming due at the first January meeting and the second ½ dues payable at the July meeting.
Persons joining during the year will be prorated accordingly. Dues-paying members have all privileges of the club, can hold an office & can cast votes.

LIFETIME MEMBERSHIP:

This class of membership will cost $300.00 one time payment at the first meeting of each year, with the option of paying $100.00 per year for 3 consecutive years (paid at the first meeting of the year in January)
If this payment plan is broken then the member shall start over on the 3-year plan, or make a 1-time $300.00 payment.

ASSOCIATE MEMBERSHIP:

This class of membership is for non- dues-paying members and they shall have all privileges of the Club except the following: He or she cannot hold an office; He or she cannot vote.

For more information regarding membership, donation & payment options, please visit the Support PCARC tab.
