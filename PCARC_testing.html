PCARC Testing

The PCARC has many accredited examiners to administer amateur radio license exams. Tests are held after our monthly meetings when necessary. Tests are administered BY APPOINTMENT ONLY. Please give our examiners two-weeks notice when requesting testing.

For more information please contact:

  • Terry Dalpoas KM5UQ: KM5UQ@yahoo.com
  • Matt Weeks W5KVV: KF5LCH@yahoo.com
What do I need to bring? What do I need to bring?
  • FCC FCC FRN Number
  • One legal photo ID (identification)
  • If no photo ID is available, two forms of identification
  • Students/minors without a photo ID need to bring only one of the above items if a legal guardian presents their photo ID
  • If applicable, bring a printed copy of your Amateur Radio license or be able to show proof of the license in the official FCC database.
  • Two number two pencils with erasers and a pen
  • A calculator with the memory erased and formulas cleared is allowed.
  • Bring a check, a money order or cash to cover the exam session fee(s).
  • Be aware that some information about you will be made publicly available on the FCC's website
