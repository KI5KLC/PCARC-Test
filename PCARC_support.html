Support PCARC

The Pittsburg County Amateur Radio Club operates on dues from its members and donations from people just like you! We are a 501(C)(3) Non-Profit Organization and all dues and donations are tax-deductible.
Club dues are $20.00 per 6 months / $40.00 per year. Monetary donations of any amount are greatly appreciated.

Donations & dues can also be taken at club meetings or mailed to:
