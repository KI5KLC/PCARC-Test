Club Meetings

Monthly meetings are held on the first Thursday of the month at 7:00 PM. Meetings take place at the Pittsburg County Emergency Operations Center. The EOC is located at 705 EOC Drive just off of West Street in McAlester, OK. Our meetings are open to the public. Anyone wishing to attend may do so.
